**Effective Date:** November 10, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Screenshot Manager, an Android application developed by DarkPhilosophy (the "App"). This
Privacy Policy
explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. By
using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Analytics and Crash Reporting

Screenshot Manager uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to improve the app's performance
and user
experience. This may include:

- Device information (e.g., device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers)
- Usage data (e.g., app interactions such as viewing, keeping, or deleting screenshots)
- Crash reports to help us fix bugs

This data is collected automatically and anonymously where possible. Firebase's privacy policy
applies to this data: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### Screenshots

The App manages screenshots stored on your device. These images remain on your device and are not
uploaded or shared unless you explicitly choose to do so through other apps or services.
